# Revolutionizing Energy with the new Sourceful Energy App for iOS and Android

Tobias Olsson
![The Sourceful Energy App is here!](https://framerusercontent.com/images/Nkbbf2FTWnAk059NXov3qG2BRFA.png?lossless=1&width=2400&height=1350)
In today’s rapidly evolving energy landscape, maximizing the benefits of your home solar power or battery system is more important than ever. That’s why we’re excited to introduce the [**Sourceful Energy App**](../), now available in for iOS users (Android coming soon). This innovative app empowers you to seamlessly connect your solar panels, energy meter and batteries to our decentralized energy network, enabling you to earn rewards, monitor your energy production, and contribute to a sustainable future.


#### Key Features of the Sourceful Energy App

• Effortless Integration:** Easily onboard your solar inverter or battery storage system to the Sourceful network without the need for additional hardware, especially if you own a SolarEdge inverter.

• **Real-Time Monitoring:** Access live updates on your solar energy production, track your energy consumption, and gain insights into your system’s performance, all from the convenience of your smartphone.

• **User-Friendly Interface:** Designed with both crypto-native and non-crypto users in mind, the app offers a walletless approach for newcomers, while allowing seamless integration with Phantom wallets for experienced users.


#### How to Get Started

1. Download the App:** The Sourceful Energy App is currently available for iOS. Visit our [app downloads page](https://sourceful.energy/app-downloads) to get started.

2. **Onboard Your System:** Follow the in-app instructions to connect your solar inverter or battery storage system to the Sourceful network. Our app supports direct integrations with major manufacturers like SolarEdge and Huawei, making the process straightforward and hardware-free for many users.


#### Join the Sourceful Community

By using the Sourceful Energy App, you’re not just optimizing your solar energy usage—you’re joining a community of technologists, innovators, and sustainable energy enthusiasts dedicated to building the world’s largest decentralized virtual power plant. Engage with us on [Discord](https://discord.com/) to connect with like-minded individuals, share ideas, and stay updated on the latest developments.

#### Looking Ahead

We’re continuously working to enhance the Sourceful Energy App, with plans to introduce features like Energy Arbitrage, which will help you buy low and use or sell high—ideal for those with battery storage. Additionally, our Android version is coming soon, expanding access to even more users.

Embrace the future of energy management with the Sourceful Energy App. **[**Download the app today**](../)** and take the first step toward a more sustainable and rewarding energy experience.
